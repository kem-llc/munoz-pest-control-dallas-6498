Overview

Our general pest control service targets the most common invaders in Dallas homes. We use a combination of interior and exterior treatments to eliminate existing pests and create a barrier that keeps them out. Monthly, bi-monthly, and quarterly plans available.

What's included

  • Eliminates ants, roaches, spiders, and silverfish
  • Low-odor treatments safe for daily life
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your routine
  • Ongoing monitoring and re-treatment if needed

Common questions

How often should I get pest control?

We recommend quarterly treatments for most homes, but monthly or bi-monthly plans are available for high-risk areas.

Are your treatments safe for pets?

Yes, we use pet-friendly products. We ask that you keep pets away during treatment and allow 30 minutes for drying.
