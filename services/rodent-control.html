Rodent Control

Humane and effective removal of rats and mice from your property.

Schedule this service

Overview

Rodents carry diseases and cause structural damage. Our rodent control program includes trapping, exclusion, and sanitation to rid your home of mice and rats. We seal entry points and provide ongoing monitoring to prevent re-entry.

What's included

  • Humane trapping options available
  • Exclusion services to seal entry points
  • Sanitation advice to reduce attractants
  • Warranty against re-infestation

How it works

1

Inspection

We identify rodent activity, droppings, and entry points.

2

Exclusion

We seal gaps and holes with steel wool and caulk.

3

Trapping & Removal

We set traps and remove captured rodents.

Common questions

How do you keep rodents from coming back?

We avoid poisons in homes with pets. Instead, we use mechanical traps and exclusion.
