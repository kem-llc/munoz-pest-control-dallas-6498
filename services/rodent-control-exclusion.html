Rodent Control & Exclusion

Remove mice and rats and seal them out for good.

Schedule this service

Overview

Rodents can carry diseases and cause structural damage. Our rodent control service includes trapping, exclusion, and sanitation. We identify entry points, seal them with durable materials, and set traps to remove existing rodents. We also offer cleanup and deodorization services.

What's included

  • Humane trapping options
  • Complete exclusion sealing
  • Attic and crawlspace services
  • Prevention plans available

How it works

1

Inspection

We locate all entry points and signs of rodent activity inside and outside your home.

2

Exclusion

We seal holes and gaps using steel wool, caulk, and metal flashing to prevent re-entry.

3

Removal

We set traps and remove dead rodents, then sanitize affected areas.
