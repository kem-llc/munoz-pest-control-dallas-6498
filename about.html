Our story

Dallas's Trusted Pest Control Since 2010

To provide Dallas homeowners and businesses with safe, effective, and affordable pest control solutions while delivering exceptional customer service.

Munoz Pest Control was founded in 2010 by Carlos Munoz, a Dallas native who saw a need for reliable, honest pest control in his community. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to quality, Carlos built a company focused on treating every customer like family. Over the years, we've grown into a team of licensed technicians serving thousands of homes across the Dallas metro area.

Today, Munoz Pest Control remains family-owned and operated. We pride ourselves on our fast response times, transparent pricing, and eco-friendly approach. Whether it's a one-time ant problem or a comprehensive termite prevention plan, we treat every job with the same dedication and care.
