Our story

Dallas's Family-Owned Pest Control Experts

Our mission is to provide safe, effective, and affordable pest control that protects families and properties in Dallas.

Munoz Pest Control was founded in 1998 with a simple goal: deliver honest, high-quality pest control to our neighbors in Dallas. Starting with just a truck and a commitment to excellence, we have grown into a trusted provider serving thousands of homes and businesses across the metroplex.

Today, we remain family-owned and operated. Our team of certified technicians undergoes continuous training to stay ahead of the latest pest control innovations. We treat every home as if it were our own, ensuring thoroughness, respect, and lasting results.
